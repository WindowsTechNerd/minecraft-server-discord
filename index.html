Target Hardware

  • ThinkPad X1 Carbon 4th Gen (X1C4)
  • Intel Skylake CPU
  • Intel HD Graphics
  • Intel Dual Band Wireless‑AC 8260 (with itlwm)
  • Intel Bluetooth (IntelBluetoothFirmware + Injector)
  • Conexant CX11852 audio (AppleALC)

Why Catalina?

Catalina (10.15) is a sweet spot for Skylake ThinkPads: stable GPU acceleration, working Intel Wi‑Fi via itlwm, Intel Bluetooth support, and functional DRM in Safari (Netflix, Apple TV+, Prime Video).
